2. Mathematical Foundations & Vector Distance Math

Unlike standard career systems that rely on hardcoded rules, this system uses Euclidean distance matching to determine career suitability. Your scores for RIASEC interests (6 dimensions) and Specialized Skills (5 dimensions) form an 11-dimensional vector. This vector is compared directly against the ideal coordinates of 25+ global career benchmarks:

d = √∑i=111 (Useri - Careeri)2 / 11

The distance is normalized to a percentage compatibility score. An Anti-Gaming consistency check evaluates rephrased questions to detect random clicking. Discrepancies apply a penalty multiplier, reducing the career suitability percentage to maintain diagnostic integrity.
